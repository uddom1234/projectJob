Website Administrator

Ultimately a Website Administrator is essential in ensuring the ___ of a website and providing an adequate user experience

Salary range: 43,000USD - 50,000USD per year

Work hours: Flexible: Minimum 40 hours a week

Key Responsiblities

  • Managing and ensuring a website is secure, functional and up-to-date.
  • Monitoring the websites performance and identify areas for improvement.
  • Collaborate with web developers, designers and content creator to ensure the company’s goals and needs and met.
  • Implements and maintain security measures to protect the website from threats.
  • Trouble shoot technical issues and errors.
  • Manage user accounts and access levels, ensuring appropriate permissions are granted.
  • Development and implement backup and recovery plans to minimize technical outages.
  • Provide support and training to website users.
  • Communicate website performance metrics to and other key information to stakeholders and position of powers within the organization.

Required Qualifications

  • Essential
    • Earn a bachelors degree in computer-related field (IT, computer science)
    • Familiar with TCP/IP, LDAP, FTP, HTTP and HTTPS
  • Preferable
    • Pursue certification in Information Technology or Computer Programming
      • CompTIA A+
      • CompTIA Network+
      • CompTIA Security+
      • CompTIA Linux+
